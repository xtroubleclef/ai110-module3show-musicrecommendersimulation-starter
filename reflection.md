# Reflection on Profile Comparisons

## High-Energy Pop vs Chill Lofi
The pop profile pushed upbeat, high-motion songs near the top, while the chill lofi profile favored lower-energy and more acoustic tracks. This makes sense because those profiles are testing opposite listening situations: "get energized" versus "stay focused."

## High-Energy Pop vs Deep Intense Rock
Both profiles rewarded high energy, so some overlap appeared, but the rock profile pulled in heavier/intense tracks that better matched mood and genre. This showed me that mood and genre still matter, but energy can dominate when it is weighted strongly.

## Chill Lofi vs Deep Intense Rock
These two profiles produced the most different outputs: chill lofi selected calm, study-friendly songs, while deep intense rock selected aggressive, fast tracks. The contrast confirmed that my preferences are actually shaping ranking direction instead of giving the same songs to everyone.
